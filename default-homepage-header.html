Passion begets innovation.

George Barley was spurred to action by his anger at the massive but avoidable damage humans do to aquatic and marine life. Find this fierce commitment to change the world within yourself, and win the prize worthy of George Barley's legacy.



The Grand Prize

The $10 million grand prize goes to the team who develops the most cost-effective, scalable technology that removes phosphorus from freshwater bodies.

First of its kind

The largest science prize ever run to focus on clean water, and is expected to attract more than 300 applicants from over 10 countries.

Science and innovation

The pursuit of a solution to water pollution. What we're after requires a brilliant marriage of proven science and cutting-edge, outside-the-box innovation.

Withstand the test of time

There are also multiple sub-prizes awarded at check-in points throughout the six year competition where each solution is tested in cold water, warm water and in a laboratory environment
